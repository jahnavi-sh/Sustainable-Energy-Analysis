# Global Energy & Sustainability Analytics

## Project Executive Summary
The transition to sustainable energy is one of the most significant challenges and opportunities for the global economy. **SustainEdge** is a data-driven initiative designed to track sustainability goals and identify opportunities for energy efficiency and cost savings. By analyzing three decades of global energy data (1990–2020), this project provides a high-level overview of energy production vs. consumption, identifies market trends for renewable investment, and monitors risk management factors across seven major global regions.

## Technical Workflow & Architecture

### 1. Data Intelligence & Metric Design
I engineered a series of high-impact Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to evaluate the efficiency of the global energy transition:
* **Energy Intensity (0.15 Index)**: Measures the energy efficiency of different economies, tracking the amount of energy required to produce one unit of economic output.
* **Renewable Share in Production (24.80%)**: Tracks the percentage of total energy production derived from renewable sources.
* **Share of Electricity (147.94 Index)**: Analyzes the electrification of global energy systems and its correlation with sustainability.
* **Supply-Demand Delta**: Visualizes the relationship between **Total Energy Consumption (Mtoe)** and **Total Energy Production** to identify regional energy dependencies.

### 2. Multi-Regional Geospatial Analysis
The project categorizes data across seven key regions—**Europe, Asia & Pacific, North America, South/Latin America, Middle East, Africa, and Arab States**—to uncover geographic disparities:
* **Wind and Solar Dominance**: Analyzed the distribution of wind and solar energy, identifying that the **Asia & Pacific** and **Europe** regions currently lead in renewable integration (33.9% and 24.75% shares, respectively).
* **Regional Benchmarking**: Created comparative visualizations to show how different regions are performing against global sustainability targets.

### 3. Temporal Trend Analysis
* **Longitudinal Studies (1990–2020)**: Implemented time-series line charts to track the steady decline in **Energy Intensity** and the concurrent rise in the **Share of Renewables** over the last 30 years.
* **Investment Spotting**: Developed trend-tracking tools to help stakeholders identify emerging markets for green energy investment based on historical growth patterns.

## Key Technologies
* **Analysis & Visualization**: Microsoft Power BI
* **Data Source**: Global Energy Transition Dataset (1990–2020)
* **Analytical Techniques**: Time-Series Forecasting, Geospatial Mapping, Efficiency Indexing
* **Domain Focus**: Sustainability, Renewable Energy (Wind/Solar), Risk Management

## Data Insights 
* **Sustainability Tracking**: Provides a standardized framework for organizations to monitor their progress toward global net-zero goals.
* **Market Intelligence**: Empowers investors to spot market trends and "Green Energy" opportunities before they become mainstream.
* **Operational Efficiency**: Identifies specific regions where high energy intensity suggests a critical need for efficiency-focused technology and consultancy services.
